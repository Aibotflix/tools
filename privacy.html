Privacy Policy

In short: this site does not collect personal data. The tools run in your browser; whatever you type stays on your device and is never sent to a server.

Data we collect

None. We do not use cookies, local storage, or analytics on this site today.

If we add advertising

In the future we may display ads via Google AdSense or a similar network. That could introduce cookies and ad personalization controlled by the ad network - not by us. This policy will be updated then, and you can manage third-party cookies in your browser settings.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard request data (such as IP address and browser type) as part of serving the site. See GitHub's privacy policy for details.
