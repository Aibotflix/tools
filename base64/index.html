Base64 Encode & Decode

Convert text to Base64 and back, with full UTF-8 support so emoji and accented characters survive. Nothing leaves your browser.

UTF-8 safe

Many naive Base64 tools use atob/btoa directly and mangle any character outside ASCII. This converter encodes through the UTF-8 byte stream first, so emoji, CJK characters and accents round-trip correctly.
