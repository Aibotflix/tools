Base64 Encode & Decode

Convert text to Base64 and back, with full UTF-8 support so emoji and accented characters survive.

Why is UTF-8 support important for Base64?

Many naive Base64 tools use atob/btoa directly and mangle any character outside ASCII. This converter encodes through the UTF-8 byte stream first, so emoji, CJK characters and accents round-trip correctly.

Is Base64 encoding secure?

Not at all. Base64 just turns binary data into text so it can be sent over text-based protocols. Anyone can decode it. It's not encryption.
