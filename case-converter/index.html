Case Converter

Convert text between every common case - UPPER, lower, Title, camelCase, snake_case and more.

What text cases are supported?

UPPERCASE and lowercase change every letter. Title Case capitalises the first letter of each word; Sentence case capitalises the first letter after a sentence-ending punctuation mark. camelCase and PascalCase strip separators and capitalise word initials; snake_case, kebab-case and CONSTANT_CASE join words with an underscore, hyphen or underscore-in-uppercase respectively.

Can I convert camelCase to snake_case?

Yes. Paste your camelCase text and click the snake_case button. It splits on word boundaries and joins with underscores. PascalCase, kebab-case, and CONSTANT_CASE all work the same way.
