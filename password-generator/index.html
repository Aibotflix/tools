Is this password generator safe?

Yes. It uses your browser's window.crypto.getRandomValues — a cryptographically secure random number generator — not Math.random(), which is predictable and unsafe for passwords. Every character is equally likely, with no bias toward any particular character.

Generation happens on your device. The password never touches a network and works offline.

How strong should my password be?

The strength meter shows roughly how many bits of entropy your settings produce. As a rule of thumb, aim for 80+ bits for important accounts. Length helps more than complexity — a long passphrase can be both stronger and easier to remember.
