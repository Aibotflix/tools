Password Generator

Strong, random passwords generated right in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere — it works offline.

—

Is this password generator safe?

Yes. It uses your browser's window.crypto.getRandomValues — a cryptographically secure random number generator — not Math.random(), which is predictable and unsafe for passwords. Every character is chosen with an unbiased algorithm, so no character is statistically favoured.

Generation happens entirely on your device. The password never touches a network, is never logged, and works with no internet connection.

How strong should my password be?

The strength meter shows roughly how many bits of entropy your settings produce. As a rule of thumb, aim for 80+ bits for important accounts. Length helps more than complexity — a long passphrase can be both stronger and easier to remember.
