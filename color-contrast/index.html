What is WCAG contrast ratio?

Contrast ratio is how much a text color stands out against its background. It goes from 1:1 (same color, invisible) to 21:1 (black on white). WCAG says normal text needs at least 4.5:1 for AA and 7:1 for AAA. Large text (over 24px or 19px bold) can get away with 3:1 for AA and 4.5:1 for AAA.

What counts as large text?

Large text is anything 24px and up at normal weight, or 19px and up for bold text. Basically if it's a heading or something big, the rules are a bit looser.
