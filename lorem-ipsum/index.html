What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin derived from Cicero's de Finibus, used by typesetters since the 1500s as dummy filler. Because it looks like real text but doesn't distract the reader with meaning, it's the standard placeholder for layout and design work.
