How do I use this thing?

Just type a color into any box. Drop a HEX like #ff6600, an RGB like rgb(255, 102, 0), or an HSL like hsl(24, 100%, 50%). The other fields and the big color swatch update as you type. You can also grab the color picker at the bottom to click something visually.
