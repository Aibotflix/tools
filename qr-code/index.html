What can I encode in a QR code?

Any text data — URLs, email addresses, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, plain text, or structured data. For URLs, just paste the full link including https://. A QR code encodes data in a 2D matrix of black and white squares. The three large squares in the corners are finder patterns that help scanners locate and orient the code.

What is error correction?

Error correction determines how much damage a QR code can withstand and still be readable. Level L (7%) is smallest, M (15%), Q (25%), H (30%) is most resilient. Higher correction = larger code.

Is the QR code generation private?

Yes. The QR code is generated entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
