Unit Converter

Convert between common units of length, weight and temperature instantly. No tracking, nothing sent anywhere.

Accurate conversions

Length and weight conversions go through a single base unit (metre and gram) using exact factors, so values round-trip without drift. Temperature is handled separately since it isn't a simple ratio. Results are shown to a sensible precision — trim trailing zeros, not accuracy.
